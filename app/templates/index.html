About FlyDrone

FlyDrone allows you to fly your drone without infringing upon any laws.

You are not allowed to fly your drone within 5 miles of an airport, near a U.S. military base, or near U.S. National Parks.

Checking all these conditions would be too tedious, so we've created an easier way to do it.
